Souvlaki with Pork Gyros

Description

Follow this hassle-free recipe for the most delicious homemade pork gyros in the air fryer!

Served in pita wraps with tzatziki sauce and fries, it’s a drool-worthy street food delicacy!

Ingredients

For creating this delicious delicacy, you will need the following:

  • 500 g pork steaks, neck, thinly cut
  • 10 g vinegar, white wine
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 2 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 1 teaspoon(s) cumin, optionally
  • ½ teaspoon(s) pepper
  • 1 teaspoon(s) paprika, smoked
  • 2 teaspoon(s) oregano, dried
  • ½ teaspoon(s) garlic, powder
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tablespoon(s) mustard

    • To serve

  • Souvlaki pita breads
  • Greek tzatziki sauce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion, slices
  • Parsley, finely chopped
  • French fries
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Put the vinegar, olive oil, salt, cumin, pepper, paprika, oregano, and garlic into a bowl.
  2. Finely chop the onion and add it to the bowl.
  3. Add the mustard, mix the ingredients with a spoon, and set the bowl aside.
  4. Place the pork chops on a cutting board and pound them with a meat tenderizer to make them very thin. Alternatively, ask your butcher to do that.
  5. Transfer the pork chops to the bowl with the marinade, mix them with your hands to marinate them well, and put them in a baking pan.
  6. Cover the pan with plastic wrap and refrigerate it for at least 4 hours. (You can leave the pork chops in the refrigerator even overnight.)
  7. Remove the pan from the refrigerator and use a pair of tongs to transfer half of the pork chops to the air fryer’s basket, making sure they are not touching each other.
  8. Cook the pork chops at 200°C(390°F) for 8 minutes on each side, and follow the same process for the remaining pork chops.
  9. Transfer the pork chops to a cutting board and finely chop them to make the gyros.
  10. Serve in pita breads along with tzatziki sauce, tomato slices, onion slices, finely chopped parsley, French fries, and mustard.
